The Pandemic nomad was created and is being updated by independent individuals living in different countries to help travellers to inform themselves about countries without having to deal with a language barrier. Therefore it might not be absolute accurat. If you notice a mistake, feel free to contact us via our contact page.

We try to update the data as soon as possible. But due to quickly changing adjustments the data might not always be up-to-date. We state the date when we updated the data of each country last time at the end of the table.

The information provided and the ability to use Pandemic Nomad is free of charge.

Please contact us via our contact form, so we can check your source and update our information if needed.
